Introduction

The National Cadet Corps(NCC) Club is a youth organization that aims to develop leadership,discipline,and a sense of responsibility among young individuals.

National Cadet Corps(NCC) Club is a platform for students to engage in various activities that promotes physical fitness,social service and national integration.

The members of the NSS play various roles such as Coordinators ,Masters, Mentors ,and Team Leaders while organizing different activities

Objectives of NCC

01. To Youth Leadership Development

02. For Discipline and Self-Reliance .

03. For Promoting National Integration.

04. For Social Service and Community Development .

05. For Preparedness for Defense and Nation Building .

06. For Personality Development .

Our Culture

Everyone in the Club is equal. One delivers to the best one's ability. No role is superior to any other role. No role is inferior to any other role.


Activists can swap or rotate their roles depending upon their interests and talents as the programs go on. So, the roles are dynamic and keep on evolving...


We believe life skills are experienced and gained. We leave no stone unturned in motivating ourselves and in promoting human values.
