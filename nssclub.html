Introduction

The National Service Scheme (NSS)Club is an integral part of building a strong connection between students and society.

NSS Volunteers engage in various activities such as clranliness drives,environmental conservation,health camps,education for underpreviliged children,and rural development programs.

The members of the NSS play various roles such as Coordinators ,Masters, Mentors ,and Team Leaders while organizing different activities

Objectives of NSS

01. To understand the community in which they work.

02. To understand themselves in relation to their community.

03. identify the needs and problems of the community.

04. To develop among themselves a sense of social and civic responsibility.

05. To utilise their knowledge in finding practical solutions to individual and community problems.

06. To develop Competence.

Our Culture

Everyone in the Club is equal. One delivers to the best one's ability. No role is superior to any other role. No role is inferior to any other role.


Activists can swap or rotate their roles depending upon their interests and talents as the programs go on. So, the roles are dynamic and keep on evolving...


We believe life skills are experienced and gained. We leave no stone unturned in motivating ourselves and in promoting human values.
