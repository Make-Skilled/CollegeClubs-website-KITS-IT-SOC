Introduction

Establishment of the Entrepreneurship Cell began with an idea of 3 students who were invited to the Global Entrepreneurship summit 2014 held at IIT Kharagpur and were inspired by the way India's prestigious institutions are supporting the overall development of engineering and management students across the country.

Empowering, cultivating, and ultimately serving those who follow you will unlock massive potential within your organization, allowing you to solve for problems in real time.

The members of the Entrepreneurship Cell play various roles such as Coordinators, Masters, Mentors, and Team Leaders while organizing different activities

Objectives of Entrepreneurship Cell

01. To Promote Creativity and Innovation

02. To Encourage Collaboration and Teamwork

03. To Provide Hands-on Learning Opportunities

04. To inspire innovation and out of box thinking

05. To Develop Problem-Solving Skills

06. To Support Networking and Skill Development

Our Culture

Everyone in the Club is equal. One delivers to the best one's ability. No role is superior to any other role. No role is inferior to any other role.


Activists can swap or rotate their roles depending upon their interests and talents as the programs go on. So, the roles are dynamic and keep on evolving...


We believe life skills are experienced and gained. We leave no stone unturned in motivating ourselves and in promoting human values.
