Introduction

A Cultural Club is a vibrant and dynamic space that brings together individuals from diverse backgrounds to celebrate,learn,and share the richness of different cultures.

The club serves as a platform for fostering mutual,respect,understanding,and appreciation of various traditions,arts,languages,and customs.

The members of the NSS play various roles such as Coordinators ,Masters, Mentors ,and Team Leaders while organizing different activities

Objectives of Cultural Club

01. To promote cultural awareness.

02. To Celebrate diversity.

03. To foster unity and harmony.

04. To encourage artistic expression.

05. To promote global citizenship.

06. To provide a Platform for Skill Development.

Our Culture

Everyone in the Club is equal. One delivers to the best one's ability. No role is superior to any other role. No role is inferior to any other role.


Activists can swap or rotate their roles depending upon their interests and talents as the programs go on. So, the roles are dynamic and keep on evolving...


We believe life skills are experienced and gained. We leave no stone unturned in motivating ourselves and in promoting human values.
